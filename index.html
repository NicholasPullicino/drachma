Our special service

Drachma is a space open to all persons of good will who seek sexual and spiritual integration

Drachma's Vision

Drachma is a group largely inspired by the Roman Catholic tradition, values and spirituality but is open to everyone. We also explore ecumenical and inter-faith spaces.

Drachma's Mission

To offer a safe space for LGBTI persons, their parents, friends and relatives where they can explore, nurture each other and grow spiritually in a holistic manner.

Multiple Spaces

Drachma gathers within it many spaces for diverse LGBTI realities. Within this larger space there are intertwining spaces for Drachma LGBTI and Parents Group.
